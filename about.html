About William Clifford

Will remembers playing around on a Commodore Vic 20 at home and on an Apple IIe at school. Although he was content with these systems, as he grew up, his family and schools continued to upgrade and he had to adapt. Since then he's been a tinkerer in many different computer platforms, operating systems, and programming languages.

Recently, Will has been in Portland Code School's Winter 2013 course. He has been learning all aspects of web-development from Ruby on Rails to HTML/CSS/JavaScript. This has been an exciting opportunity. With his classmates, he has worked on the following projects:

Cards Against Humanity War

A online game of Cards Against Humanity in which you can vote on the “best” Cards Against Humanity hands.

FreeCycle-PDX Mapper

Will is on the Dev team of a project trying to plot offerings from FreeCycle-PDX mailing list to Google Maps.

PreKix

Will is on the Stakeholders team of a project trying to develop an app to assist people with KickStarter campaigns.

Will also loves writing and telling stories. At college, in addition to a few programming courses, he also studied Literature, Writing, and Journalism. Over the years, he has been involved in a variety of writing groups, public-access television shows, theatre stunts, role-playing games, and other creative endeavors. He has not yet found a way to combine all these things, but he is very intent on figuring that out.
